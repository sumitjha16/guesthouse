Celebrated Destinations You'll Adore !

  • Marina Beach (Approx. 8 km) - Scenic urban beach with a vibrant atmosphere.
  • Kapaleeshwarar Temple (Approx. 9 km) - Historic temple known for its stunning Dravidian architecture.
  • San Thome Basilica (Approx. 10 km) - Roman Catholic basilica built over St. Thomas's tomb.
  • Elliots Beach (Besant Nagar Beach) (Approx. 9 km) - Serene and less crowded beach with calm waters.
  • Guindy National Park (Approx. 7 km) - Protected area with diverse wildlife.
  • Semmozhi Poonga (Botanical Garden) (Approx. 12 km) - Lush botanical garden with a variety of plant species.
  • Valluvar Kottam (Approx. 10 km) - Memorial dedicated to classical Tamil poet Thiruvalluvar.
  • Fort St. George (Approx. 13 km) - Historic fort housing the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly and museum.
  • Cholamandal Artists' Village (Approx. 18 km) - Artists' commune and gallery showcasing vibrant art exhibitions.
  • DakshinaChitra Cultural Center (Approx. 15 km) - Living museum showcasing South Indian art and lifestyles.
